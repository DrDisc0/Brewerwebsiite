Meet Chuck Brewer

Chuck Brewer

Established in 1992 while Chuck Brewer was finishing up his bachelor's degree in Architecture from Lawrence Technological University he started helping friends, family and acquaintances with architecture and interior design services, while working at a local design firm that did exclusively commercial projects. C Brewer & Associates produces both residential and commercial projects working with clients directly, ownership groups, landlords, management companies and contractors. We can provide "as built" conditions, schematic design, space planning, design development, construction documents, project management and consulting throughout the project. Working with each client to meet their individual needs, as efficiently as possible has proven very successful. Many clients have used C Brewer & Associates for numerous projects.
